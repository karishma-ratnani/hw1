Our Story

Established in 2004, Honey Bubble was founded to bring the traditional Taiwanese drink of boba tea to the diverse and thriving city of Atlanta. We serve up a variety of delicious hand-crafted beverages, from classic teas to fruit smoothies and slushes. With over 100+ options, there is something new to try every day!
